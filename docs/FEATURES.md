# Elite v1 — Feature Catalog (by role)

Benchmarked against Keeta / Talabat / AliExpress, focused on Newtech's vertical: electronics + installation + delivery. ✅ = v1 scope, 🔜 = fast-follow.

## 🛍️ Customer (web + mobile)
- ✅ Phone-OTP login (Kuwait), guest browsing, profile, multiple Kuwait addresses (governorate/area/block/street/building).
- ✅ Rich catalog: categories, brand pages, variants (color/model), media galleries, specs, related & "frequently installed with".
- ✅ Search with typo tolerance + filters (brand, price, category, "needs installation").
- ✅ Product detail with **"Buy + Install"** toggle (adds installation fee + scheduling).
- ✅ Cart, promo codes, free-delivery threshold (10 KWD), order summary in KWD.
- ✅ Checkout: address → delivery slot → optional installation slot → KNET / Apple Pay / Google Pay.
- ✅ **Live order tracking**: status timeline + real-time driver map (like Keeta), ETA.
- ✅ Installation tracking: scheduled window, technician on the way, before/after photos, sign-off.
- ✅ Order history, reorder, digital warranty card per item, invoices (Zoho).
- ✅ Support chat + warranty claims in-app (Zoho Desk backed).
- ✅ Push/SMS/email notifications for every milestone.
- ✅ Ratings & reviews. 🔜 Wishlist, loyalty points, referrals, AI product assistant.

## 🚚 Driver (mobile)
- ✅ Login, availability toggle (online/offline), assigned zone.
- ✅ Task queue (today's deliveries), accept/reject, optimized sequence.
- ✅ Turn-by-turn navigation, **live GPS streaming** to customer & admin.
- ✅ Proof of delivery: photo + recipient name + OTP/signature.
- ✅ Cash-on-delivery collection reconciliation. 🔜 Earnings, route optimization, batch pickup.

## 🔧 Technician (mobile)
- ✅ Login, availability, skill/zone profile.
- ✅ Installation job queue with scheduled windows, customer address & map.
- ✅ Job flow: en route → arrived → in progress → completed, with **checklist**, before/after photos, customer signature.
- ✅ Parts/notes capture, reschedule request. 🔜 Inventory of parts, knowledge base, video assist.

## 👷 Employee / Operations (web)
- ✅ Order management: search, status changes, refunds (audited), notes.
- ✅ Catalog & inventory management: products, variants, media, stock by location.
- ✅ Manual dispatch & reassignment of delivery/installation tasks.
- ✅ Customer service: tickets queue, chat, warranty handling.
- 🔜 Bulk import/export, purchase orders.

## 🧑‍💼 Admin (web)
- ✅ Full operations dashboard: revenue, AOV, conversion, top products, live order map.
- ✅ Inventory health, low-stock alerts, demand by area.
- ✅ Driver/technician utilization, delivery & installation SLA, support SLA.
- ✅ Role & staff management (invite, assign zones), audit log.
- ✅ Pricing, discounts/campaigns, free-delivery rules.
- ✅ **Marketing**: push Newtech catalog to Meta product feed, create/monitor campaigns & audiences (Meta Ads).
- ✅ **Finance**: invoice/payment/expense sync to Zoho Books.
- 🔜 Forecasting, A/B pricing, cohort analytics.

## Platform-wide
- ✅ Arabic-first RTL + English, instant language switch.
- ✅ Realtime everywhere (orders, GPS, jobs, chat).
- ✅ Unified notification center (push/email/SMS/in-app).
- ✅ Offline-tolerant field apps (queue + sync).
- ✅ Secure by RLS; audited privileged actions.
- 🔜 AI layer: product recommendations, support copilot, demand forecasting, smart dispatch.

## How we beat the benchmarks
- **vs Keeta/Talabat:** same world-class live tracking & UX, but for *electronics + professional installation* — a unified order→deliver→install→warranty→support thread no food app models.
- **vs AliExpress:** local (Kuwait) inventory, 24h delivery, real installation by vetted technicians, real warranty & in-person support — not cross-border shipping.
- **The moat:** one relational model where commerce, logistics, field service, warranty, and support are all first-class and realtime — plus tight local payment (KNET) and ops integrations (Zoho).
